About Sarah

I've run the companies you're running.

Most of my clients are small business owners who are genuinely great at what they do. The financial side is where things get complicated — and where a lot of money quietly walks out the door. I've watched it happen from the inside: vendor invoices that miss early-pay discounts, processing fees that add up on transactions that didn't need to cost that much, financial statements that nobody ever sat down and actually explained.

That's the part I love about this work. Getting into the details, finding what's being missed, and giving you a clear picture of where your business stands — and where it's going.
