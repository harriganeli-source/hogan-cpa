About

Sarah Hogan,
Owner & CPA

With more than 18 years of experience in financial and administrative management within the industrial and manufacturing sectors, Sarah brings a practical, results-driven perspective to every client engagement. After earning a Bachelor's degree from Nicholls State University and a Master of Business Administration from Tulane University, she built a successful career leading financial operations, improving processes, and supporting organizational growth across complex business environments.

In 2019, Sarah transitioned into public accounting. Today, her firm provides a comprehensive range of services, including tax preparation, bookkeeping, financial compilation, and fractional CFO support. She specializes in providing the financial insight and strategic guidance necessary for clients to make informed, high-level decisions.

Multi-District Litigation Support

Beyond traditional accounting, Sarah offers specialized Multi-District Litigation (MDL) support services, with a primary focus on the Northern District of Florida. She assists legal teams with the rigorous financial analysis and documentation required for complex litigation — work that draws directly on her background navigating sophisticated financial environments.

The client-side advantage

Having spent nearly two decades on the client side of financial management, Sarah understands the challenges business owners face and provides solutions that are both technically sound and operationally practical. She is dedicated to building long-term relationships through responsive, personalized service for clients throughout Florida and beyond.
