About

Sarah Hogan,
Owner & CPA

Before opening Hogan CPA Firm, Sarah spent nearly two decades not auditing businesses from the outside, but running them from the inside — managing financial operations, overseeing vendor relationships, controlling cash flow, and keeping companies moving through the industrial and manufacturing sectors. She holds a Bachelor's degree from Nicholls State University and an MBA from Tulane University, and she's used both every day of her career.

She knows what it looks like when nobody's actually watching the numbers. When a vendor invoice slips past its payment terms and the early-pay discount disappears. When a business is running on credit cards instead of ACH and losing a percentage on every transaction. When the financial statements exist but nobody ever translated them into something the owner could use to make decisions. That's the work Sarah does — not just preparing returns and closing books, but being the person in your back pocket who understands your business and tells you the truth about it.

Who she works with

Sarah's clients are mostly service businesses — landscapers, CRNAs, anesthesiologists, law firms, and clinics across the Gulf Coast. Small and mid-size operations where the owner is exceptional at what they do and needs a financial partner who can handle everything else. She offers tax preparation, bookkeeping, financial compilation, fractional CFO support, and specialized MDL litigation services.

Why responsiveness is the whole thing

The number one reason people leave their CPA is that they can't get a response. Sarah built her entire practice around the opposite. She answers her phone. She responds to emails. She takes the anxiety and fear out of dealing with the IRS — and out of dealing with your accountant. That's the firm she wanted to work with, so it's the firm she built.

"Sarah has been a trusted advisor to me for many years. While her accounting and tax expertise are exceptional, what I value most is her ability to understand the realities of running a business.
