About Sarah

A CPA who has sat where you sit.

Before opening Hogan CPA Firm, Sarah spent nearly two decades on the client side — leading financial operations, improving processes, and supporting growth for companies in the industrial and manufacturing sectors. She knows how decisions get made inside a business, not just how to report them afterward.

Today her firm offers tax preparation, bookkeeping, financial compilation, fractional CFO support, and specialized services for Multi-District Litigation cases. She works with business owners, executives, and legal teams who need more than a number-cruncher.
