Litigation

MDL Litigation Support

Multi-District Litigation consolidates complex civil cases before a single federal judge for coordinated pretrial proceedings — reducing duplicative discovery, avoiding conflicting rulings, and conserving resources. A critical component of most MDLs is the Common Benefit Fund, which compensates attorneys whose work benefits all plaintiffs. Federal judges increasingly rely on CPAs to oversee this process with the independence and rigor it demands.

Common Benefit Fund Administration

Establishing and managing the systems that track attorney time, evaluate expense submissions, and administer common benefit funds — transparently and in accordance with court orders.

Accounting & Auditing

Independent financial controls and reporting that give courts and litigants confidence that assessments are handled fairly, accurately, and with full accountability.

Litigation Support

Financial analysis and documentation prepared to the standards complex federal proceedings require — from discovery through settlement administration.
